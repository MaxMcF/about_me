THREE TRUTHS AND TWO LIES!!!

As a story-loving person, I love listening to other people's interesting stories, so I made a program to play a guessing game that gives people the platform to tell their stories.

Welcome to the best gameshow in the world!! Its three truths and two lies, the game where you must answer all questions correctly or else you FAIL. Remember there are only three true statments and two false statements, so pick carefully.
